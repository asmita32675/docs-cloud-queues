Pricing and service level
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Cloud Queues is part of the Rackspace Cloud and your use of the Cloud
Queues API is billed based on the `Cloud Queues pricing schedule`_.

Information about the 
`Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Cloud Queues`_ is available in the 
`Rackspace Legal Information`_.

.. _Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Cloud Queues: http://www.rackspace.com/information/legal/cloud/sla#cloud_queues
.. _Cloud Queues pricing schedule: http://www.rackspace.com/cloud/queues/
.. _Rackspace Legal Information: http://www.rackspace.com/information/legal/cloud/sla#cloud_queues.
